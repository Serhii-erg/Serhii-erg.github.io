About Me

Welcome to my portfolio! I am Serhii Serdiuk, a professional with a diverse academic and professional background in Human-Centered Design (HCD), User Experience, Computer Simulation, and Human-Computer Interaction (HCI). I have extensive experience in both research and teaching, having worked on various projects and courses.

Projects

IST grant "Teaching Basic Chemistry to Students at Risk" at Penn State

Led the development of an Intelligent Tutoring System architecture, which included expert models and knowledge bases, aimed at enhancing the learning experience and assessment methods for students.

IST 197G: Technical Vocabulary for Information Sciences and Technology

Developed and taught this course focusing on the essential technical vocabulary required for students in the field of Information Sciences and Technology.

Human-Machine Interface (HMI) for Process Control Systems

Collaborated with ETHA company on developing HMI for process control systems in the production of building materials at the Bershad plant in Ukraine, emphasizing ergonomic system design and HCD.

Ergonomic Assessment of Automated Control Systems

Applied discrete-event modeling techniques for ergonomic assessment of control system operators at JSC aviation enterprise "Motor-Sich" in Ukraine, determining optimal composition of process equipment and operator interfaces.

Intellectual Support of HMI Ergonomic Design

Contributed to a DAAD grant project at Rostock University (Germany) focusing on the ergonomic design of HMI using SCADA systems tools, applying AI methodologies for complex challenges in HCI.
