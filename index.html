About Me

Welcome to my portfolio! I am Dr. Serhii Serdiuk, a professional with a diverse academic and professional background in Instructional design, Human Factors, Human-Centered Design, User Experience, Human-Computer Interaction. I have extensive experience in both research, teaching, creating online courses and interactive educational resources.

Projects

IST grant "Teaching Basic Chemistry to Students at Risk" at Penn State

  • Goal: Develop a gamified Tutoring System architecture to increase student engagement and improve student assessment in basic chemistry courses.
  • My Role: Co-Principal Investigator and lead game scenario designer, responsible for designing interactive and engaging learning experiences.
  • Tools: Unity, JavaScript, HTML/CSS, Canvas LMS, Adobe Creative Suite.
  • Design and Development Methods: Comprehensive quiz system (500 questions), interactive modules, gamification techniques, instructional design principles, user testing and feedback incorporation.
  • Results: Achieved 85% positive feedback from 370 students enrolled in Chemistry 110 and 130 at Penn State, demonstrating significant improvement in engagement and understanding of course material.
  • Examples: Project Overview

IST grant "Teaching Basic Chemistry to Students at Risk" at National University "Zaporizhzhia Polytechnic"

  • Goal: Develop an Intelligent Tutoring System architecture to enhance learning and assessment methods for students at risk in basic chemistry.
  • My Role: Lead developer and designer, responsible for the overall project vision and execution.
